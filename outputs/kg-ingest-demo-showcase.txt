SCENARIO 4  -  Same quest, different account types (the divergence)
======================================================================

Doric's Quest has a Mining 15 requirement modelled as an OR:
'be a main (just buy/trade the bar) OR have Mining 15 (self-source it)'.
Watch the same quest answer differently by account type:

   main                     (family=main   ) -> no extra requirement (a main just buys it)
   ironman                  (family=ironman) -> must self-source: Mining 15
   ultimate_ironman         (family=uim    ) -> must self-source: Mining 15
   hardcore_group_ironman   (family=ironman) -> must self-source: Mining 15

   -> The engine faithfully reports BOTH OR-branches; the advisor/UI layer
      hides 'or be a main' from an ironman (it's not an action they can take).
