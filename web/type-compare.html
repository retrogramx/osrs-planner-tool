My lean: Option C. Use Bold 12 for identity + section headers + stat numbers — it IS the in-game interface voice, so the account mirror reads as authentically "the game." Reserve Quill for a few brand/ledger flourishes (the app title, a tagline). Pure-Quill everywhere can feel storybook; pure-Bold-12 everywhere can feel rigid. The split gives you authentic UI and the ledger soul. But this is your taste call — tell me which column wins.
